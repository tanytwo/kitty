Project Ideas

Overview

The project idea that I have revolves around making a 2D game with simplistic and minimalistic features. The game will be easy to understand and enjoyable for anyone, from people who are not heavily invested in gaming to casual gamers to hardcore gamers. The idea of the game will be based around a continuous and long running game or never-ending game to keep players constantly coming back. The project would involve a lot of designing and catering towards a user-centred design, making it clean and aesthetically pleasing to the eye would be one of the main objectives.

Motivation

The project will be interesting as many individuals are starting to get into the trend of “aesthetically pleasing” things and “minimalistic” designs. With the rise of technology, people are spending more and more time on their devices, even leisurely activities would be conducted on these devices. Through creating this game, it will capture the attention of many individuals through the game’s simple and attractive design and keep the audience through its intuitive gameplay mechanics.

Description

The game will be in a visually clean cut 2D style with soft colours and a minimalistic design, catering towards people into the latest trends of minimalism and modern representation. The game could potentially be a simple platforming game, a collection game that takes real time or a simple and repetitive role-playing game.

Tools & Technology

Software and hardware needed for the development of this project would include any type of personal computer or laptop and a gaming developing tool such as Construct2. Other programs needed would be Photoshop for developing and refining art and designs for the game.

Skills Required

The skills required for this project would be the ability to understand and configure a simple game and the ability to design and create concept art for the game and be able to put them together into digital form.

Outcome

If the project is successful, the outcome of this project would be an enticing game for many. The game could be used as an easy way to kill time or a leisure activity.
