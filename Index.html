Interest in IT

My interest in IT started during my last few years of high school, with the stem of my interest coming from the childish dream of becoming a game or character designer. As I became increasingly involved with one of the most played online games ever, League of Legends, my interest in technology and design fluctuated.

During VCE I did Visual Communication Design and through extracurricular classes held by my college, I was able to experience basic and simple coding with the use of “Scratch” a free programming language. These events helped peak my interest into the world of IT.

I chose to start my higher education at RMIT due to its accessibility and how friendly its community and environment it is. I also have many friends who are already studying at RMIT and they can help guide me if I ever need them.

While studying at RMIT I expect to be thoroughly knowledgeable in the fundamentals of Information Technology.
